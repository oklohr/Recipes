# Guacamole
#cooking/recipe #2020/February# #cooking/for large groups# #cooking/side dish#
- - - -
[Guacamole Recipe - Allrecipes.com](https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/14231/guacamole/)

## Ingredients
2 avocados
1 roma tomato
1 lime
Garlic salt

## Process
Dice tomato.

Mash avocado with lime and garlic salt.

Fold in tomato.
